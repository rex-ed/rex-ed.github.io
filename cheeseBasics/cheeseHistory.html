A Brief History of Cheese

Once upon a time there was a farmer who lived alone. Every morning he would wake up early to milk his cows, but since it was the olden days he didn't have a fridge to put the milk in.

So after a while he ended up with this insane surplus of warm, spoiling milk that he kept in open jars around his room.

One day he was sick in bed, coughing and sneezing everywhere. His gross, germy bacteria filled the room and started eating the old milk, making it clump and solidify.

When he got better from the cold, the farmer, the disgusting old bachelor that he was, ate the hard milk anyway.

And thus, cheese was born.
