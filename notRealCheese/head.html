Head Cheese

This stuff is nasty.

It's not even close to cheese, but they called it head chhese because it was the only name gross enough for this abomination of a food.

It's chopped up little peices of a pig's head that we normally wouldn't eat (for good reason) that are then boiled and set in aspic jelly.

That's right, its a loaf of jellied pig head.
