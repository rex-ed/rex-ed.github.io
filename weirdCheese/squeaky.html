Squeaky Cheese

There's this Finnish cheese that you eat with jam for dessert that makes this fun squishy-squashy squeaking sound when you chew it, and all my life everyne in my family has called it Niirsku Naarsku Juusto, so logically, that's what I thought it was called.

But it turns out that that's just a silly name that my Great-Grandmother came up with!

Apparently Niirsku Naarsku is just the sound that it makes and no one else calls it that!!

It's actually called Leipajuusto, which just means bread cheese.

My life is a lie.
